Your club's results and standings, live

A clean, modern results and championship standings site for club racing and karting — built for the volunteer running timing on a laptop trackside, not a pro series with a media department.

Grassroots racing deserves better than a Facebook post

Club and karting series still run on decades-old timing software, printed result sheets, and someone hoping to remember to update a spreadsheet.

🖥️

Results trapped on one laptop

Your timing export lives in one official's Orbits install. If they're not at the next event, nobody can find last month's results.

📄

PDFs and printed sheets

Drivers and families refresh a Facebook group hoping someone posts a photo of a printout taped to the timing trailer.

🧮

A standings spreadsheet, maybe

Championship points live in a spreadsheet someone updates when they remember — if the formula didn't break three rounds ago.

Upload a file. Get a site.

No IT skills required — designed for a volunteer with 20 minutes between heats.

Upload your timing export

Drop in an Orbits export, map your own timing software's columns if it's not Orbits, or enter results by hand if there's no file. Nothing gets lost — the original file is stored before anything is even read.

Review what we read

See exactly what was parsed, fix anything that looks off, and add rows manually if needed. You publish, not a black box.

Publish and share

Get a clean, mobile-friendly results page with a shareable link — the kind you'd actually text a driver's parent.

Everything a club season actually needs

Not just a results page — the whole championship, start to finish.

🏆

Real championship standings

Points schemes with drop rounds, countback tiebreakers, fastest-lap bonuses, and penalties — computed live from your results, not a spreadsheet someone has to remember to update.

🏁

Multi-class sessions

Two classes sharing track time in one race? Split results by class in the same session, each with its own field and standings.

🙋

Driver profiles

Every driver gets a public page with wins, podiums, and results history across clubs — and can claim it as their own with just their email.

👥

More than one admin

Invite other club officials to help run the season. Nobody's results are trapped on a single volunteer's laptop.

🔔

Email alerts

Subscribe to a class or a driver and get an email the moment new results or standings publish — no more refreshing a Facebook group.

🖨️

Export & print

Download results and standings as CSV, or print a clean handout for the trophy table — straight from the same page everyone already bookmarks.
